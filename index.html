Research

RectifiedHR: High-Resolution Diffusion via Energy Profiling and Adaptive Guidance Scheduling

A research project focused on improving high-resolution image quality generated by latent diffusion models (Stable Diffusion 1.5) through energy profiling and adaptive guidance scheduling. The work analyzes latent energy evolution across denoising steps and proposes rectified/adaptive guidance schedules for producing visually and quantitatively better high-res images.

Stable Diffusion Energy Profiling Adaptive CFG High-Resolution

Local Prompt Adaptation for Style-Consistent Multi-Object Generation in Diffusion Models

Research on local style diffusion models that enable fine-grained control over image generation by applying style transformations to specific regions while maintaining global coherence. This work explores techniques for localized style transfer and region-aware diffusion processes.

Style Diffusion Local Control Region-Aware Image Generation
Code Paper

NeRF Few-Shot Limitations: Analysis of Neural Radiance Fields in Low-Data Scenarios

Investigation into the limitations and challenges of Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF) when trained with limited training data. This research analyzes the performance degradation, generalization issues, and potential solutions for few-shot NeRF training scenarios.

NoPropagation: Backpropagation-Free Deep Learning

Implementation of the No-Propagation Diffusion Transformer (NoPropDT) from scratch in PyTorch. This project demonstrates training deep models using local layer-wise targets and diffusion-based denoising without traditional backpropagation, achieving ~99% accuracy on MNIST and ~76% on CIFAR-10.

PyTorch Diffusion Models Transformers Deep Learning
